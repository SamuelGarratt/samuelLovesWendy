For my dear Wendy

Returning from work

To me o my Wendy you are so caring
And with my bad humour you're also forbearing
Always planning, and working, getting things done
Yet at a days end, you're still so much fun

When I arrive home from a tedious working day
'tis my joy such nice food in yummy array
And receive such fresh welcome, to me you cheer
To see your sweet smile and your flowing hair

Sweet relationship

My lovely Wendy, to me so friendly
I love you my dear without any fear
We're living together, and raising our bub
we're changing her nappies, to burp her back rub

You bear with so much, e'en sweat covered sheet
yet still introduce me, your friends I do meet
So patient you are, I often forget
with multiple to dos, I don't do the net

You still understand, my hand you still hold
together with you into one we will mold
Working together, each other we care
And in life's stillness we're lovingly near
